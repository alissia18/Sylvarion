Week Eight

Hacker Culture

Yes, I would help the scientists distribute the vaccine around the world once it is confirmed safe. However, this will not be an easy battle: since capitalism reigns supreme currently, the side with the most money almost always wins the fight. This does not mean it is impossible: Aaron Swartz and Demand Progress managed to stop SOPA (Stop Online Piracy Act)’s passage through congress, and I believe I could take inspiration from his methodology. Yes, I help them. First, I would suggest to the scientists that we form an online group committed to making vaccines available worldwide to abolish the pandemic once and for all, similar to how Aaron founded Demand Progress. Then I would suggest that everyone contact as many other research scientists as they know and pitch the project to them as well, so that we could get more traction all around the world. I would then convince the scientists to share the vaccine formulas with the online group so that they can start producing the vaccines in their own countries.

Once we had our own vaccine in production, I would start forming petitions and extending the outreach to the general public announcing that we have the vaccine cheaply available. In doing all of this, mine and the scientists’ goal would not be to make money. It would be to make the world a better place and I think we can all agree that eradicating COVID would do just that. Through public outreach such as rallies, posters and blog posts, we could convince the general public to boycott Omnicorp’s vaccine. We wouldn’t have to worry about shipping as much either because we have representation in research facilities around the world.

This is a dangerous endeavour and we may be in danger of being prosecuted by federal law enforcement; because of his moves to make information public, Aaron Swartz was facing 13 felonies. However, they did amend the article which was the basis for most of those felonies after his death, so it’s likely that we wouldn’t be in as much danger as he was. This cause is more than worth the risk; succeeding at free vaccine distribution would be a victory for the entire world.
